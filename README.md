# Early Warning System for Academic Depression

## Overview

This project aims to identify students who may be at risk of academic depression using Machine Learning and Data Analytics techniques. The system analyzes academic, lifestyle, and mental health-related factors to predict depression risk and provide actionable insights through an interactive Power BI dashboard.

## Problem Statement

Academic depression is influenced by multiple factors such as academic pressure, financial stress, sleep habits, dietary habits, and mental health conditions. Early identification of at-risk students can help educational institutions provide timely support and intervention.

## Objectives

* Predict student depression risk using Machine Learning.
* Analyze factors contributing to academic depression.
* Visualize trends and risk indicators through Power BI dashboards.
* Support data-driven decision-making for student well-being.

* Students reporting suicidal thoughts show higher depression risk.
* Increased academic pressure is associated with higher depression levels.
* Financial stress significantly impacts student mental health.
* Poor sleep duration contributes to increased depression risk.
* Unhealthy dietary habits are linked to higher depression prevalence.
